How the Platform Works

The Smart Club Management System platform is designed as a centralized hub for students to learn, collaborate, and stay connected with the campus coding community.

Members can log in to access learning resources, stay connected with the campus coding community, and track coding contests and workshops.

Club leaders manage the platform through a dedicated dashboard to organize events, manage members, review project submissions, and monitor student participation, creating a digital ecosystem for growth and innovation.
