How the Platform Works

The GeeksforGeeks Campus Club platform is designed to support learning, collaboration, and innovation among students in the coding community.

Members can log in to explore learning resources, follow structured learning paths, participate in coding events, and submit project ideas to be published in the Community Showcase.

Club leaders manage the community through a dedicated dashboard where they can organize events, review project submissions, and monitor student participation.
